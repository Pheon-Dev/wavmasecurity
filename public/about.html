About us

Wavma Security Company Limited

A duly incorporated and registered under the companies Act, laws of Kenya. As a limited company, it transacts business as laid down in the companies Act Chapter 486 laws of Kenya. The company is fully authorized and licensed to conduct business in Kenya having setup and started operations in year 2019 and we continue to grow our operations. The board of directors is drawn from a wide field of experience in the service industry, security, finance, accounting, human resource and administration. The senior management consist of individuals with experience in the service and security industry. Further, we have a team of qualified operational and training staff who can handle and deliver to required standards.
